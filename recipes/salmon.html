oven baked salmon

Description

A delicious and juicy oven baked salmon covered with mayonaise and lemon.

what do i need?

  • salmon 700g
  • mayonaise 300ml
  • 1 lemon
  • salt
  • pepper
  • dill

How to prepare?

  1. preheat oven to 170 degrees
  2. place salmon fillet in a large oven safe pan
  3. season fillet with salt and pepper to taste
  4. cover with slices of lemon
  5. cover everything generously with mayonaise and cover dish with tinfoil
  6. cook 30 minutes then remove tinfoil and cook another 15 minutes
  7. cut to portions and serve with smashed potatoes or cooked veggies
