Pesto pasta

Description

creamy pesto pasta with chicken

what do i need?

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • dried italian seasoning
  • kosher salt
  • butter
  • chicken broth
  • cooking cream
  • parmesan
  • mozarella cheese
  • basil pesto
  • penne pasta
  • salt and pepper

how to prepare?

  1. Brown chicken and onion in a pan with italian seasoning
  2. melt butter and add garlic. cook for 30 seconds
  3. add the chicken broth, heavy cream, parmesan cheese and mozarella to pan. mix and cook for 5-10minutes
  4. cook pasta according to package instructions
  5. mix together the chicken,pasta and pesto sauce. stir to combine and cook until warmed through.
  6. season with aditional salt and pepper
  7. garnish with additional parmesan cheese and enjoy!
