Korean Beef Stirfry

Description

A delicious korean style spicy beef stirfry

What do i need?

  • 750g thinly sliced beef(5mm thickness)
  • 50ml soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 4 gloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of grated ginger
  • 2 tablespoons of gochujang(korean red pepper paste)
  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame seeds

How to prepare?

  1. in a medium bowl mix beef,soy sauce, garlic, gochujang,sesame oil and ginger
  2. place in ziploc bag and marinade for 3 hours
  3. preheat cast iron pan over medium-high heat
  4. add the mix to pan and cook in a single layer for 2-3minutes per side
  5. serve immediately and garnish with choped green onion and sesame seeds
